Overview

The Imperial Rebels of [Classified] (IROC), later renamed South Classified, was a short-lived rebel nation inspired by [Classified]. The IROC
was founded by Giri, the same person who founded [Classified] initially.

        
History

The IROC was founded on November 17th, 2024, by Giri (Ronaldosuiii49 at that time).
