Overview
        
The Food Empire of Scratch is an alliance of four food-themed nations. It is currently the largest alliance in Scratch Politics,
though it is currently inactive.
 

        
History

The idea of a "Food Empire of Scratch" was first mentioned on May 19, 2024, the day the Eggs fell under Popcorn control, by @NickNackTheTitaniac,
as seen in the image below:
        


However, it wasn't until October 3rd, 2024 that the FEOS was actually created. 


        
Member Nations

The Food Empire of Scratch currently has 3 member nations, ranked by followers.

1. The Great Egg Army
2. The Popcorn Uprising
3. The Apple Empire
4. The Sour Patch Army
