Overview
        
The SPRK is a vast collection and archive of the history of Scratch Studio Wars. It originally stood for "Scratch Politics Record Keeping",
however it is not officially an abbrevation of that anymore.
 

        
History

On July 20, 2025, MasterX shared a Scratch project by the name of "buriasan info". It contained one entry about Buriasa. It may be found here.
Later, MasterX unshared the Scratch project, as he felt that a Scratch project was too limiting for something intended to be a massive archive. He later created a GitHub repository.
He used GitHub Pages and created the "Buriasan Wiki", which was eventually intended to integrate into a future Buriasan website. It still only had one page about Buriasa.
A few days later, at an unkown date, MasterX renamed the Buriasan Wiki to the "SWRK", which was an abbrevation for "Scratch Wars Record Keeping". It was now being endorsed by Sable Keystone.
Later, with Giri's recommendation, MasterX renamed the SWRK to the "SPRK", its final name. SPRK was an abbrevation for "Scratch Politics Record Keeping".
By mid-September 2025, MasterX was permanently banned from Sable Keystone and Buriasa, as a result of serious tensions. The SPRK then became a project of Details.
By March 2026, the SPRK had grown into multiple pages, instead of just one. On May 18, 2024, the SPRK became an independent entity seperate from Details, although it
still uses a Details subdomain at https://sprk.vkrc.net/.
